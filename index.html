Scala Sans is a Dutch typeface de-

signed by Martin Majoor in 1993 to

be compatible with his serifed Scala.

It is often described as a 'humanist'

typeface with a softer personality than

that of Akzidenz Grotesk, Futura, or

Helvetica. Scala Sans is easy on the

eyes and delights designers with its

x-height. Flaunting the perfect duo of

edgy lines and sassy curves it ap-

peals to designers' keen eye for a

modern look while hinting at an

old typographic style underneath.
